ABOUT ME

As a Civil Engineering graduate with a passion for visual arts, I specialize in bringing structural designs to life through both technical rigor and creative expression. My expertise spans civil engineering software like AutoCAD and ETABS, ensuring precise and sustainable infrastructure solutions.

Beyond engineering, I leverage advanced skills in Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and Lightroom for professional photography, video editing, and visual documentation. This dual capability allows me to not only design resilient structures but also communicate their vision and impact through compelling visuals.

Driven by innovation and a collaborative spirit, I am committed to contributing to impactful projects where engineering acumen meets artistic flair.
