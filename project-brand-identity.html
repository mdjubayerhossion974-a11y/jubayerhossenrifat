Project Details

Eternal Beauty is a modern beauty brand created to celebrate both outer elegance and inner confidence. The brand focuses on products that enhance natural charm while inspiring self-love and empowerment. With a clean, timeless identity, Eternal Beauty combines minimal design and meaningful storytelling, reflecting a vision where beauty goes beyond appearances.

  • Brand guideline creation
  • Mockup presentations
